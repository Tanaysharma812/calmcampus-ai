💡 The Problem

Many students suffer silently from mental health issues like stress, anxiety, and loneliness due to academic and social pressure. There is a lack of accessible, personalized, and culturally engaging mental health support systems.

🚀 Our Solution

MindConnect is a hybrid (online + offline) support platform that connects students with verified mental wellness institutes, offers personalized healing through activities and events, and focuses on cultural exchange and community building.
